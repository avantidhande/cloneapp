    New double-glazed sashes

       Whether you would like to upgrade from single glazed to double-glazed, you always have the option            to do so without replacing the whole frame. If the current box frame is in good condition, there is no             reason to remove it. Going doubled glazed can help fix condensation problems and these new sashes        are also always designed like for like to match the original windows.


        


         

    Overhaul and draught-proofing

       Making drastic improvements on keeping the warmth inside your home, reducing noise,smooth                  running sashes & no more rattling on those windy days. The staff and parting beads are replaced for          ones with integral draught excluders fitted inside. Then, we would fit an integral draught excluder on           the bottom sash meeting rail, making the whole window completely sealed. A new fastener is fitted on          at the end to pull both the top and bottom sashes together creating more of a seal.
